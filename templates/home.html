Quick information about raccoons

What they like to eat: (1)
  • Fleshy Fruits
  • Grains
  • Invertebrates
  • Rodents
  • Fish
Habitat: (1)

"Raccoons prefer wooded areas near streams, ponds,
and marshes but are highly adaptable and can live
in agricultural areas and in proximity to human
developments."

"They make their dens in tree cavities,
abandoned woodchuck or fox burrows, rock crevices,
brush piles, chimneys, attics, sheds, and other structures."

Why the name "Trash Panda"? (2)

"From a likening of the black patches around a raccoon's eyes to similar markings on a giant panda,
it being in the same superfamily as the red panda,
and a reference to its tendency to forage for garbage in urban environments."
